MWANZA CAMPUS

Welcome to the City College of Health and Allied Sciences Mwanza Campus, a branch of CCOHAS with NACTE Registration Number REG/HAS/212. We are proud to be part of a network of City Colleges.

VISION

TO BECAME A CENTER OF EXCELLENCE FOR TRAINING, RESEARCH AND ENTREPRENEURIAL TRAINING IN MEDICAL,PHARMACEUTICAL AND BIOMEDICAL SCIENCES TO SUPPORT SUSTAINABLE AND DEVELOPMENT OF HEALTH AND INDUSRIAL SECTOR IN TANZANIA

MISSION

THE MISSION OF THE COLLEGE IS TO PROMOTE THE GOOD HEALTH OF THE COMMUNITY BY PRODUCING HIGHER CALIBER HEALTH PROFESSIONAL, PROVIDE ENTREPRENEURIAL TRAINING IN HEALTH CARE, PHARMACEUTICAL AND BIOMEDICAL SCIENCES AND BY SUPPORTING RESEARCH HERE BY TO SUPPORT SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT OF HEALTH AND INDUSTRIAL AND SOCIO-ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT OF TANZANIA.

OBJECTIVES

  • SOLVE HEALTH RELATED PROBLEMS OF THE COMMUNITY THROUGH OPERATIONAL RESEARCH.
  • IMPROVE QUALITY OF HEALTH IN THE COUNTRY IN PROVIDING ESSENTIAL CLIENT CARE.
  • RELATE HEALTH OF THE COMMUNITY TO SOCIO-ECONOMIC AND POLITICAL DEVELOPMENT IN PROVIDING HEALTH CARE SERVICES.
  • IMPROVE THE HEALTH AND WELL-BEING OF CLIENTS WITH FOCUS TO THOSE AT HIGH RISK AND AT CRITICAL CONDITION.
  • RECOGNIZE LIMITS OF COMPETENCE AND REFER SUCH ISSUES WHICH REQUIRE HIGHER LEVELS OF COMPETENCE.
  • SUPPORT SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT OF PHARMACEUTICAL INDUSTRIAL AND INDUSTRIAL SECTOR IN GENERAL.

VALUES

IN ORDER TO REALIZE THE VISION AND FULFILL ITS MISSION, CCOHAS SHALL BE GUIDED BY THE FOLLOWING VALUES

  • (I) INTEGRITY – TO ACT ETHICALLY, HONESTLY, AND WITH FAIRNESS.
  • (II) RESPECT – TO LISTEN, VALUE, AND ACKNOWLEDGE OTHERS.
  • (III) COURAGE – TO LEAD, TAKE RESPONSIBILITY, AND QUESTION.
  • (IV) EXCELLENCE – TO STRIVE FOR EXCELLENCE AND DISTINCTION.
  • (V) IMPACT – TO EMPOWER, ENABLE, AND INSPIRE.
